Our Story

Bringing Kenyan Cuisine to the Fine Dining Table

Savanna Grill was born in 2019 from a simple idea: Kenyan food deserves a seat at the fine dining table. Founded by Chef Amani Odera, a Culinary Institute of America graduate who returned to Nairobi with a mission, the restaurant reimagines classic Kenyan dishes with modern techniques and presentation — without losing the soul of the food.

Our ingredients are sourced from passionate farmers across Kenya. The tilapia comes fresh from Lake Victoria. The organic vegetables are harvested from farms in Limuru. The single-origin coffee is roasted from beans grown in Nyeri. We firmly believe the best flavours come from the land, not from a corporate supply chain.

What started as a modest 20-seat pop-up in Westlands has grown into one of Nairobi's most talked-about dining experiences. We now seat 80 guests across our ground-floor dining room and our open-air rooftop, offering beautiful views of the Ngong Hills.

Our Values & Philosophy

Local Sustainability

We build direct relationships with local farmers and fishermen, ensuring fair compensation and reducing our ecological footprint by utilizing what Kenya's soil offers.

Culinary Innovation

We respect heritage while refusing to be limited by it. Our kitchen constantly experiments with new textures, forms, and combinations to elevate traditional flavours.

Warm Kenyan Hospitality

To us, every guest is family. We deliver professional, world-class fine-dining service wrapped in the genuine warmth and joy of true Kenyan hospitality.
